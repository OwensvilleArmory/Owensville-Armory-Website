Can I have a gun sent here?

Found a gun online? You can have it sent here. We ask that you notify us so we know who it belongs to.

Can you order guns?

We are happy to order in anything that you need. If we can get it we will order it.

Do you offer payment plans?

We offer layaway with minimum 20% down and 90 days to pay.

Do you handle NFA items?

We do not deal with NFA items at the moment. We are planning on it in the future.

Do you do gunsmithing?

We are equipped for some jobs such as replacing parts or other small repairs. We can do almost everything on ar-15 pattern guns.

Do you accept checks?

We do not accept any checks. Some Exceptions for B2B and LE apply.

Do you charge for background checks?

Background checks are free. We don't belive a service free for us should cost you anything.

Do you carry live bait?

No, we currently do not have the room or equipment to handle live bait.
